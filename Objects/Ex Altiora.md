A book from the library of [[Jurgen Leitner]]
Mentioned in [[Episode 4 - Page Turner]], [[Episode 46 - Literary Heights]].

**Description:**
Hand-bound book, with a leather binding.
The book is completely in Latin.

**Has woodcut illustrations of:**
about a dozen illustrations, mostly mountains and cliffs. One appeared to be a tower, looming over the surrounding countryside at an odd angle, with tiny birds just visible circling the summit. As well as a picture of an empty sky.

**Points of Interest:**
Reading the book triggers people's vertigo.
