Statement of [[Timothy Hodge]], regarding his sexual encounter with one [[Harriet Lee]] and her subsequent death.

**Date of statement:** [[December 9, 2014]].

**Points of Interest:**
He met [[Harriet Lee]] at a bar. She seems to be looking around the bar nervously.
While they left the bar, she tightly held on to [[Timothy Hodge]]'s arm. She also starts scratching her arms. Her nervousness stopped when they made it to Timothy's place.
[[Harriet Lee]] is a student. From [[Salisbury]], but left for mysterious reasons.
A long time ago, she said she saw a Woman in a red dress lying on the ground. When she got close, the woman ran to her, grabbed her, felt a stabbing pain in her stomach, then fell unconscious.
The [[Woman in Red Dress]] had [[Black Hair]].
[[Harriet Lee]] keeps seeing the [[Woman in Red Dress]] everywhere she goes.
Something under [[Harriet Lee]]'s skin starts squirming. [[Timothy Hodge]] left the room and called an ambulance, then he heard "a sort of wet, cracking thump." He made his way to the dark room, he said he couldn't describe what he saw. ([[Hard to Describe Creatures]])
The room was littered with [[White Worms]].
