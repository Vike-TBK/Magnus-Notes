Statement of [[Julia Montauk]], regarding the actions and motivations of her father, the serial killer [[Robert Montauk]].

**Date of Statement:** [[December 3, 2002]]

**Points of Interest:**
Dad's a killer. Killed at least 40 people over the course of 5 years, Before his arrest in [[1995]]. He died in prison in [[2002]].
A book was made about [[Robert Montauk]]. "[[No Bodies in the Shed]]" by [[Ray Cowan]].
[[Julia Montauk]] was 12 when this happened.
[[Julia Montauk]] lived in [[York Road, Dartford]].
The weird stuff started happening when the mom died. After returning home from watching [[The Witches]], she heard thumping and everything was dark and stuff. The kitchen light was on, but no one was home. The back door was open.
The Mom's pendent was on the table. It's a silver abstract shape of a hand, with the symbol of a closed eye. ([[Strange Eye]])
Mom's gone. Dad's asleep, when she woke him, he saw the pendent in [[Julia Montauk]]'s hand, and when downstairs to the kitchen.
The kitchen taps started leaking a dark, brackish-smelling liquid. ([[Dark Liquid]])
Dad told her to go to bed. He probably didn't sleep.
In the morning, [[Robert Montauk]] returned home, looked pale. He said mom's gone. (Shame we don't know what happened that night.)
[[Robert Montauk]] started spending time in the shed in the backyard. He's gone most of the time, even at night. (Probably something related to what happened to the mom.)
One night when she was pretending to sleep, the dad laid his hand on her. It smelled like blood and brackish water.
He promised "It wouldn’t get me too."
A few years later, the phone rang. There was an old man with a etrange accent on the other side, who wanted speak with [[Robert Montauk]]. The man on the other side said it was [[Detective Rayner]] on the other side, and he had a new case for him. ([[Joseph Rayner]], perhaps? Or someone related?)
Dad came in a took the phone. He paled when he heard what she told him about what the man said. He told her leave, but she went to replace a broken light bulb. She overheard him say, angrily "No, not already. Do it yourself." Then he went very quiet and listened, before finally he said okay, that he’d do it as soon as possible, then hung up.
[[Robert Montauk]] started taking photographs and filling many canisters. She suggested making a darkroom to develop the photos, and so he turned the guest bedroom into one, but told her to not go in. So she went in and saw photos of dead people, with strange symbols painted on their faces. ([[Strange Symbols]])
She told her father she when in. He gave he a long hug, said it would all be over soon, and left.
Time: 2:47 am
That night, she didn't sleep. The lights outside started going out.
"I heard a knock on the front door. Firm, unhurried and insistent. Silence. I did not move. The knocking came again, harder this time, and I heard the door rattle on its hinges. As it got louder it began to sound less and less like a person knocking and more like… wet meat being slammed the study wood of the front door."
[[Julia Montauk]] called the police.
The door broke down and she heard growling. She ran to the shed. Blue light came from it. Inside it was [[Robert Montauk]]. He was knelt in the centre of an ornate chalk pattern scrawled on the floor. In front of him lay an unknown dead man with his chest cut open. In one hand [[Robert Montauk]] held knife, and in the other he held the man’s heart. [[Robert Montauk]] was chanting and as the song rose and fell the heart in his hand beat to its rhythm, and the blue light brightened and dimmed in time. the walls were covered in shelves filled formaldehyde containing a single heart, which also beat in time with the one in [[Robert Montauk]]’s hand.
The dead man had the same silver pendant as the mother.
[[Robert Montauk]] stabbed the man's heart, and the blue glow, the darkest, and growling vanished.
They heard sirens in the distance, [[Robert Montauk]] said he was sorry, and ran away.


**Points of Interest From Jonathan Sims:**
The dead man's name is [[Christopher Lorne]].
The heart jars were arranged on the walls on the individual shelves forming patterns of eleven hearts on each inner wall and seven on the wall with the door.
Cult name for the pendent: [[Peoples’ Church of the Divine Host]], a small cult that grew around the defrocked Pentecostal minister [[Maxwell Rayner]]. Name seems to be a coincidence with [[Joseph Rayner]].
[[Christopher Lorne]] was apart of this cult, and hasn't been seen in six years before his murder.
[[Maxwell Rayner]] disappeared in [[1994]] and his cult fragmented shortly after.
[[Robert Montauk]] died in prison in [[November 1, 2002]]. He was stabbed 47 times and bled out before anyone found him.
No culprit or weapon was found. He was alone in his cell and was supposedly locked.
The light bulb in his cell had blown out.
