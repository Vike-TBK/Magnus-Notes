Statement of [[Melanie King]], regarding what they saw at the abandoned [[Cambridge Military Hospital]] whilst filming in [[January, 2015]].

**Date of Statement:** [[April 17, 2016]]

**Case Number:** #0161704

**Points of Interest:**
[[Andy Caine]], [[Peter Warhol]], [[Antonia Farron]].
Peter didn't come.
They hired [[Sarah Baldwin]] to cover for him.
Call went to voicemail. Unknown number called as [[Sarah Baldwin]].
> "She was short, really short, with dark brown hair cropped close. She was wearing black cargo trousers and a grey T-shirt, with a light black jacket over the top."

She smoked a lot. She had a sharp, floral perfume.
[[Sarah Baldwin]] did like the building.
Graffiti on the wall says, “Silk will not stitch the butcher’s meat”. ([[Butcher]])
[[Sarah Baldwin]] hadn't slept, but she wasn't tired.
Sarah wandered off. [[Melanie King]] took a night vision camera to look for her.
She could smell something,
> "It smelled of copper, with another scent beneath it, acrid and sour, with the faintest hint of ammonia." (Apparently that's the smell of a rotting corpse)

> "Sarah was there, with her bag next to her. She was gesticulating wildly and talking, though I couldn’t see anyone else in the room with her. I couldn’t make out most of it, though I heard the words “trespass” and “unintended” several times, and whispered apologies. I was almost about to call out to her, when she suddenly stood bolt upright. It seemed like she was struggling to breathe. Then I heard an impact, and Sarah seemed to be flung across the room by a heavy blow. She hit the wall hard, and I heard a crack as she slumped down, a smear of blood left on the wall."

[[Sarah Baldwin]] said something in an unknown language, and peeled off the skin of her left arm. Underneath was something dark and shiny. Then she stretched it out, put it back on, then stapled it on. (This could be [[Creature with Long Limbs]])
After the filming, they dropped [[Sarah Baldwin]] off and never saw her again.

[[Jonathan Sims]] asked if she was dreaming. She got defensive.
